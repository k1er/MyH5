Privacy Policy

AI Repeater
Last Updated: January 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Privacy First: AI Repeater is designed with privacy at its core. All AI transcription processing happens locally on your device using WhisperKit technology. Your audio files and transcriptions never leave your device.

We believe that your language learning journey is personal and private. This privacy policy explains how AI Repeater handles your data with the utmost respect for your privacy.

Data Processing

Local Processing Only

AI Repeater uses on-device AI technology to provide transcription services:

  • Audio Files: All audio files you import are stored locally on your device. They are never uploaded to any server.
  • AI Transcription: Speech-to-text conversion is performed entirely on your device using WhisperKit, Apple's local AI framework.
  • Learning Data: Your vocabulary lists, favorites, and learning progress are saved only in your device's local storage.
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not provide cloud synchronization services, ensuring your data stays on your device.

Device Requirements

The app requires devices with A13 Bionic chip or later to perform local AI processing. This ensures fast, private, and efficient transcription without requiring internet connectivity.

Information We Collect

Crash Analytics

AI Repeater uses Firebase Crashlytics to help us improve app stability and performance:

  • Purpose: To detect and diagnose crashes and technical issues
  • Data Collected: Device model, OS version, crash logs, and app version
  • Personal Information: No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected
  • Usage: This data is used solely to improve app stability and fix bugs

No User Accounts

AI Repeater does not require user registration or account creation. We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • User credentials or passwords
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking

Data Sharing and Third Parties

Zero Data Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

AI Repeater integrates the following third-party service:

  • Firebase Crashlytics: Operated by Google LLC, used solely for crash reporting. Crashlytics data is processed according to Google's privacy policy.

No other third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used in this app.

Your Data Rights and Control

Complete Data Control

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete Anytime: You can delete individual audio files, transcriptions, or vocabulary lists at any time within the app
  • Complete Removal: Deleting the app from your device will permanently remove all local data
  • No Recovery: Since we don't store data on our servers, deleted data cannot be recovered
  • Opt-Out: You can disable crash reporting in the app settings if desired

Data Retention

All data is retained only on your local device for as long as you keep the app installed. There is no server-side data retention.

Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is stored in your device's secure app sandbox
  • Access to app data is protected by iOS security features
  • Local processing eliminates the security risks associated with data transmission

However, please note that no device is completely secure. We recommend using device passcodes and keeping your iOS updated.

Children's Privacy

AI Repeater is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect personal information, we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Parents and guardians can safely allow children to use this app for language learning.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We will notify users of significant changes through:

  • App Store update notes
  • In-app notifications
  • Updates to this page

Continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

Legal Basis for Processing

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and other regions with data protection laws:

  • Local data processing is based on the performance of the app's core functionality
  • Crash analytics data is processed based on legitimate interest in maintaining app quality
  • You have the right to object to crash data collection in app settings
