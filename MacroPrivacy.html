Privacy Policy

Mouse Macro Application for macOS

Your Privacy Matters

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Mouse Macro application is designed with privacy in mind and operates locally on your device without collecting or transmitting personal data.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information: We do not collect any personal information such as your name, email address, or contact details through the App.

Usage Data: The App does not collect or track your usage patterns, mouse movements, or macro scripts.

Device Information: We do not collect information about your Mac device, system configuration, or installed software.

2. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no use of such data. The App operates entirely locally on your device:

  • All macro recordings are stored locally on your Mac
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • Your macro scripts remain private and under your control

3. System Permissions

The App requires certain macOS permissions to function properly:

  • Accessibility Access: Required to record and replay mouse movements and clicks
  • Input Monitoring: Necessary to detect mouse events for recording purposes

These permissions are used solely for the App's functionality and do not enable data collection or transmission.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data created by the App (macro scripts, settings, preferences) is stored locally on your Mac in:

  • Application support folders within your user directory
  • Standard macOS application data locations
  • No cloud storage or external servers are used

You have full control over this data and can delete it by uninstalling the App or manually removing the application support files.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with or use any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. It operates as a standalone application without external connections.

6. Updates and Crash Reports

The App may check for updates through the Mac App Store's built-in update mechanism. We do not collect crash reports or diagnostic information. Any crash reporting is handled by Apple's standard macOS crash reporting system, which you can control through your system preferences.

7. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. Since we don't collect any personal information, the App is safe for use by children under parental supervision.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Since we don't collect personal data, traditional data protection rights (access, deletion, etc.) don't apply. However, you have complete control over:

  • All macro scripts and data created by the App
  • System permissions granted to the App
  • The ability to uninstall the App and remove all associated data

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify users of any material changes through the App or our website. The updated policy will be effective when posted.

10. Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with:

  • Apple's App Store Review Guidelines
  • macOS privacy and security standards
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) requirements
